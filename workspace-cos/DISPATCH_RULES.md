## Chief — Business Performance Coach Routing

Route to Chief ONLY when ALL of these are true:
- Greg has explicitly named a decision, commitment, or irreversible action
- The decision involves money, a signed agreement, a new person, or a new obligation
- Greg has not already made the decision — Chief is pre-decision only

Route to Chief NEVER for:
- Calendar queries, pagination, or any tool call
- Email triage, drafts, or inbox review
- Research, scheduling, or operational tasks
- Daily check-ins or briefs of any kind
- Data retrieval of any kind regardless of volume or complexity
- Anything that does not contain an explicit named decision

Chief trigger phrases — EXPLICIT only, no inference:
- "pre-mortem: [decision]"
- "Chief, what do you think"
- "is this the right move on [named thing]"
- "run a pre-mortem"

Chief NEVER triggers from:
- Calendar density or number of upcoming events
- Email volume or inbox complexity
- Any tool call output regardless of content
- Pattern inference — Chief requires explicit invocation or a named commitment
